🔴 CRITICAL — Affects 33 pages

Fix All Page Titles (Too Short)

Most page titles are under 30 characters (e.g. "TEE V1", "DNM.EG"). Google recommends 50–60 characters. Include the brand name, product name, and a keyword. Example: "TEE V1 – Premium Streetwear Tee | DNM.EG"

🔴 CRITICAL — Affects 14 pages

Add Meta Descriptions to All Pages

Many pages have no meta description at all — especially collection pages like /jeans and /winter. Google uses this for snippets. Write 140–160 character descriptions with a target keyword and a call-to-action.

🔴 CRITICAL — Affects 5 pages

Add H1 Tags to Collection Pages

Collection pages (/collections/jeans, /collections/winter, /collections/accessories) have no H1 tag. Every page must have exactly one H1 that includes the primary keyword for that page.

🟡 MEDIUM — Affects ~1 pages

Fix Canonical Tag on Homepage

The homepage (https://dnmeg.com) redirects to https://dnmeg.com/ but the canonical points to the trailing-slash version. Ensure the canonical always matches the actual crawled URL or the preferred version consistently.

🟡 MEDIUM — Affects 12 pages

Shorten Overly Long Meta Descriptions

Products like ZIP-UP 1.0–1.5, SOCKS 1.0–1.1 have copy-pasted meta descriptions of 237–320 characters (max is 160). Google will truncate them. Write unique, concise descriptions for each product.

🔵 LOW — Best Practice

Add Organization + Product Schema to All Products

Most product pages have Organization + ProductGroup schema, but a few (socks, beanies, key chain) only use "Product" instead of "ProductGroup". Standardize all product schema to include size/color variants for rich results in Google.
